How the bot works

🔍

SMT Divergence

Compares NQ and ES to detect when one makes a new high/low that the other fails to confirm — a key institutional signal.

📊

Fair Value Gap

Detects price imbalances (FVG/IFVG) where institutions left unfilled orders, marking high-probability entry zones.

💧

Liquidity Sweep

Identifies when price sweeps session highs/lows to trigger stop orders before reversing — the classic ICT entry model.

📈

BOS / MSS

Break of Structure and Market Structure Shift confirmation ensures the bot only enters after trend direction is confirmed.

⏰

ICT Window Filter

Only trades during 9:00–12:00 AM New York time — the highest-probability session for NQ institutional flow.

📋

Trade Journal

Every paper and live trade is logged to CSV automatically with entry, exit, PnL, setup type, and timestamps.

Frequently asked questions

Does it trade real money automatically?

Not by default. The bot runs in paper trading mode until you explicitly connect a broker API and enable live mode. You are always in control.

What data feed does it use?

Currently uses Yahoo Finance for NQ and ES futures prices (1-minute delayed). Upgrade to Tradovate or Databento WebSocket for real-time tick data.

What's the Master ICT Setup?

The bot only enters when all four signals fire simultaneously: a liquidity sweep, SMT divergence between NQ and ES, a fair value gap, and a break of structure. This high confluence requirement keeps trade frequency low and quality high.

Can I run it outside of 9–12 AM?

Yes — the ICT window filter can be adjusted in your .env file. The default 9–12 AM window follows ICT's "New York AM session" methodology.
